About this market

I have closed two transactions in Yuba City — this is a market I know personally.

Yuba City is a market I know personally — I closed a buyer transaction at 648 Forbes Ave in December 2023 and another buyer transaction at 1134 Plumas St in April 2023. It offers something increasingly rare in California: genuine affordability paired with a strong local economy. The healthcare sector anchors the employment base with Sutter Medical Center as a major employer, and the agricultural heritage gives the community a rootedness you don't find in faster-growing suburbs. For buyers priced out of Placer County who want to stay within reach of Sacramento, Yuba City deserves serious consideration.
