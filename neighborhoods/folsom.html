About this market

Folsom is where families stay forever.

Folsom is consistently ranked among the best cities in California to raise a family — and for good reason. The school district is exceptional, Folsom Lake is minutes away, the historic district on Sutter Street has genuine charm, and the community pride here is real. I have helped buyers find homes in Folsom and know the market well. It sits just outside Placer County in Sacramento County but serves as a natural extension of the Placer County corridor for many of my clients.
