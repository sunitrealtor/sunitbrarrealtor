Market overview

What homes look like
in Lincoln.

Lincoln is the fastest-growing city in Placer County and one of the strongest value propositions in the entire Sacramento region. Families who want more space — a real backyard, an extra bedroom, room for extended family — consistently find that Lincoln delivers what other communities can't at the same price point.

I recommend Lincoln most often to relocating families with children who want newer construction and to buyers whose extended family members are considering Sun City Lincoln Hills. Having multiple generations close together in the same region is something I understand personally.

The commute to Sacramento is longer than Rocklin or Roseville — typically 45–55 minutes to UC Davis — so Lincoln works best for professionals with flexible schedules or those working in north Placer County.
