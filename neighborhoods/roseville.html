Why Roseville

The complete package
in Placer County.

🏥
Sutter Roseville Medical Center
One of the region's leading hospitals is right here in Roseville. For physicians joining Sutter Health, living in Roseville means a 5–15 minute commute to work.
💼
Major employers nearby
HP and Oracle are both headquartered in Roseville. The city has a strong tech economy that continues to attract educated professionals and remote workers from the Bay Area.
🛍️
Best shopping & dining
Roseville has the most diverse retail and restaurant scene in Placer County. Westfield Galleria, dozens of locally-owned restaurants, and everything in between.
🎓
Highly ranked schools
Roseville City and Roseville Joint Union school districts are both highly rated. Woodcreek High School, Junction Elementary, and Diamond Creek Elementary are standouts.
🏘️
Neighborhood variety
West Roseville near Hwy 65 has newer master-planned communities. East Roseville near Hwy 80 offers established neighborhoods with mature trees and larger lots.
📍
Central location
Right in the center of Placer County. Easy access to Rocklin, Lincoln, and Granite Bay. 35–45 minutes to UC Davis. Sacramento Airport is 25 minutes away.
