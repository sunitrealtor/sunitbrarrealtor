Common questions

What people usually ask first.

Do I need to be ready to buy to reach out?
Not at all. Many of my best client relationships started with a casual conversation months before anyone was ready to move. There's no pressure and no obligation — just information and honest advice.
Do you work with buyers relocating from out of state?
Yes — and this is one of my specialties. I relocated here from Ohio myself, so I understand exactly what long-distance buying feels like. I offer video tours, neighborhood walkthroughs by video call, and full coordination for buyers who can't visit in person before making an offer.
Do you only work in Placer County?
I'm most active in Placer County — Loomis, Granite Bay, Rocklin, Roseville, and Lincoln — but I also serve Folsom, Yuba City, Marysville, and Plumas Lake. If you're not sure whether your area is covered, just ask.
Can you help with commercial real estate too?
Yes. I've closed retail and office leases in addition to residential transactions. Most agents don't work both sides — I do. If you're a business owner looking for space or an investor exploring commercial opportunities, I'm happy to help.
What's the best way to reach you quickly?
Call or text me directly at (216) 288-2096. I respond to texts quickly and am available seven days a week. If you prefer email, reach me at sunitrealtor@gmail.com.
